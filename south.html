Starters

Masala Dosa

A famous South Indian crispy rice and lentil crepe filled with spiced potato mixture

Idli

Soft and fluffy steamed rice cakes, served with coconut chutney and sambar

Vada

Crispy deep-fried lentil fritters, served with coconut chutney and sambar

Medu Vada

Larger, donut-shaped lentil fritters, served with coconut chutney and sambar

Bonda

Deep-fried potato or lentil dumplings, a popular tea-time snack

Uttapam

Thick savory pancakes made from fermented rice and lentil batter, often topped with vegetables

Paneer Tikka

Marinated and grilled cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) with spices and yogurt

Chicken 65

A spicy and tangy deep-fried chicken dish, a popular non-vegetarian starter

Fish Curry

South Indian-style fish curry, often served as an appetizer or main course with rice

Gobi Manchurian

Fried cauliflower florets tossed in a spicy and tangy sauce, an Indo-Chinese fusion dish

Main Course

Sambhar

A tangy and spicy lentil stew with vegetables, served with rice, idli, or dosa

Rasam

A flavorful tamarind and tomato soup, often served with rice

Curd Rice (Thayir Sadam)

Rice mixed with yogurt and tempered with spices, a soothing and simple dish

Chicken Curry

A rich and spicy chicken curry, a staple in South Indian non-vegetarian cuisine

Chettinad Chicken

A spicy and aromatic chicken curry prepared with Chettinad spices

Avial

A mixed vegetable dish cooked with coconut and yogurt, often served with rice

Lemon Rice

Tangy and flavorful rice dish with lemon juice, turmeric, and mustard seeds

Biryani

Fragrant rice dish with marinated meat or vegetables and aromatic spices

Pongal

A comforting dish made with rice and lentils, typically garnished with ghee, cashews, and black pepper

Desserts

Payasam

A creamy and sweet rice pudding flavored with cardamom and garnished with nuts and raisins

Kheer

Rice pudding made with milk, sugar, and garnished with almonds and pistachios

Gulab Jamun

Deep-fried milk-based dumplings soaked in sugar syrup, a popular Indian dessert

Jalebi

Spiral-shaped deep-fried sweet pretzels soaked in sugar syrup, often enjoyed as a dessert

Mysore Pak

A rich and sweet fudge made from gram flour, ghee, and sugar

Badusha

Flaky and sweet fried dough soaked in sugar syrup, similar to a glazed donut

Rasgulla

Soft and spongy cottage cheese balls soaked in sugar syrup, a classic Indian dessert

Coconut Ladoo

Sweet coconut and condensed milk balls, easy to make at home

Halwa

Various types of halwa, such as semolina (rava) halwa or carrot halwa, are popular South Indian desserts

Pootharekulu

Paper-thin and delicate sweet rice and jaggery sheets, a specialty from Andhra Pradesh
