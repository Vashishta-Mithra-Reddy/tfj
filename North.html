Specials Starters Main Course Desserts

Starters

Samosa

Deep-fried pastry filled with spiced potatoes or minced meat

Pakora

Fritters made from vegetables (e.g., onion, potato, spinach) or paneer, coated in chickpea flour batter

Aloo Tikki

Potato patties spiced with various seasonings and often served with chutneys

Paneer Tikka

Marinated and grilled cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese)

Tandoori Chicken

Chicken marinated in yogurt and spices, cooked in a tandoor (clay oven)

Tandoori Seekh Kebab

Minced meat (usually lamb or chicken) mixed with spices, skewered, and cooked in a tandoor

Chaat

A popular street food dish with a variety of ingredients like chickpeas, potatoes, yogurt, and chutneys

Pani Puri/Golgappa

Hollow, crispy balls filled with spicy tamarind water, potatoes, and chickpeas

Dahi Puri

Similar to Pani Puri, but filled with yogurt, tamarind chutney, and spices

Hara Bhara Kabab

Vegetarian kababs made with spinach, peas, and spices

Main Course

Butter Chicken

Chicken cooked in a creamy tomato-based sauce

Chicken Tikka Masala

Grilled chicken pieces in a rich tomato and cream-based sauce

Rogan Josh

A fragrant and spicy curry made with tender pieces of meat (usually lamb or goat)

Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer cubes in a creamy tomato-based gravy

Chole Bhature

Spiced chickpea curry served with deep-fried bread (bhature)

Dal Makhani

Creamy black lentils cooked with butter and spices

Rajma

Red kidney beans cooked in a thick tomato gravy

Aloo Gobi

Potatoes and cauliflower cooked with spices

Palak Paneer

Cottage cheese (paneer) cooked in a creamy spinach gravy

Mutton Biryani

Fragrant rice cooked with spiced mutton and aromatic spices

Chicken Biryani

Fragrant rice cooked with spiced chicken and aromatic spices

Desserts

Gulab Jamun

Deep-fried milk-based dumplings soaked in sugar syrup

Rasgulla

Spongy cottage cheese balls in sugar syrup

Kheer

A creamy rice pudding flavored with cardamom and garnished with nuts

Jalebi

Deep-fried swirls of batter soaked in sugar syrup

Gajar ka Halwa

Sweet carrot pudding cooked with ghee, milk, and sugar

Rasmalai

Spongy cottage cheese patties served in a creamy saffron-flavored milk

Malai Kulfi

Creamy Indian ice cream with flavors like pistachio, saffron, and cardamom

Shahi Tukda

Fried bread slices soaked in sweetened milk and garnished with nuts

Phirni

A rice pudding made with ground rice, milk, and sugar, flavored with cardamom and saffron

Ghevar

A disc-shaped sweet dessert made from flour and soaked in sugar syrup
